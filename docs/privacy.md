# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-03

Welcome to Trispend ("the App"). This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, how we store/share it, and the choices you have.

## 1. Information We Collect

Trispend does **not** require an account, registration, or sign-in. We do not collect your name, email, or any other identifying information.

- **App Data**: Your trips, expenses, categories, and participants — all stored locally on your device.
- **Purchase Info**: In-app purchase/entitlement status (e.g., Pro subscription, expiration date) processed via the App Store. We do **not** store your credit card number.

> **Note**: We do not collect analytics data, crash logs, or usage statistics. We do not use accounts, and we do not sell your personal data.

## 2. How We Use Information

- Provide and maintain core features (tracking expenses, splitting, analytics).
- Process subscriptions/purchases and restore entitlements.
- Fetch up-to-date currency exchange rates.
- Legal compliance and abuse prevention.

## 3. Storage & Sync

- **Local Storage**: Your data is stored on your device via **Apple SwiftData** in a local database.
- **No Cloud Sync**: The app does NOT sync your trip or expense data to any server or iCloud. All data remains on your device unless you manually back up via device backup.
- **Data Portability**: Pro users can export their data in **CSV and PDF** formats for backup purposes.
- **Deletion**: Deleting the app will remove all local data. Device backups may retain copies depending on your backup settings.

## 4. Subscriptions & In-App Purchases

- We use the App Store / StoreKit to process purchases and entitlements.
- Pro is offered as a **monthly** or **annual** auto-renewing subscription, or as a one-time **lifetime** purchase.
- Auto-renewing subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled **at least 24 hours** before the current period ends. The lifetime purchase is a one-time payment and does not renew.
- Restore Purchases will restore your Pro entitlement on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 1) Firebase (Cloud Firestore)
We use Google's Firebase Cloud Firestore **solely to fetch up-to-date currency exchange rates**. This is a read-only lookup; we do **not** send your trips, expenses, email, or any personal data to Firebase. We do not use Firebase Authentication, Analytics, or Crashlytics.

### 2) Apple (refund-assist data, only if you request a refund)
If you request a **refund** for an in-app purchase made in Trispend, we may share necessary data with Apple about your **access/consumption** of the purchased content (e.g., unlock time, whether it was used) **solely** to help Apple assess the refund request, following Apple policies and the data-minimization principle.

## 6. Your Choices & Rights

- **No Account Required**: Because the app does not use accounts, your data is never tied to an online identity.
- **Marketing Opt-out**: We currently do not send marketing communications; if introduced, we will provide an opt-out.
- **Regional Rights**: We honor privacy rights under applicable laws (e.g., access and deletion).
- **Data Access & Deletion**: All your data lives on your device. You can delete any trip or expense within the app, and uninstalling the app permanently removes all local data.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Trip and expense data stored on your device is retained until you delete it via the app or uninstall the app.
- **Purchase Records**: App Store transaction records are retained by Apple per their policies. We only store entitlement status (Pro/Free) locally on your device.

## 8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately.

## 9. International Transfers

We do not collect personal data, so no personal data is transferred across regions. Exchange-rate lookups are served by Google's global infrastructure and contain no personal information. We take reasonable measures to protect any data in transit.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you in-app or on our site about material changes. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 11. Contact Us

For any questions or privacy requests, contact: trispendapp@gmail.com
